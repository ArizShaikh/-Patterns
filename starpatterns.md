# -Patterns


Aim : Program to display the following types of '*' patterns 
        (a) Square pattern
        (b) Forward slanting line
        (c) Backward slanting line
        (d) Triangle (Forward and backward)
        (e) Pyramid

Description : 
        (a) Square pattern
                        * * * *
                        * * * *
                        * * * *
                        * * * *
                        
        (b)  Forward slanting line 
                        *
                          *
                            *
                              *
        (c) Backward slanting line
                                *
                              *
                            *
                          *  
        (d) Triangle 
                   *                            *
                   *  *                      *  *
                   *  *  *                *  *  *
                   *  *  *  *          *  *  *  *
                 
        (e) Pyramid
                                *
                               * *
                              * * *
                             * * * * 
                            * * * * *
